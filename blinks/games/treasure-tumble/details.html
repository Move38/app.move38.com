Instructions

SETUP Take your sushi roll case and lay it out on a flat surface. This will be the floor of your treasure chamber. Long press 2 Blinks until they flash white, then set them standing up on the sushi roll case. These are your treasure buckets. Take another Blink and set it between the two buckets. This trio of Blinks is the foundation of your treasure chamber. Finally, take at least 1 Blink and triple click it. It should now be green with a swirling face. This Blink is your treasure spawner. It will drop one treasure piece whenever it is placed on top of the treasure chamber tower.

The starting setup for Treasure Tumble

GAMEPLAY The goal of this game is to collect as much treasure in your bucket as possible by using the mechanisms built into the treasure chamber walls. The game is played in two stages. First, both players work together to assemble the treasure chamber. Take turns adding one Blink at a time to the tower and watching the mechanisms self-assign. There are four kinds of mechanisms that will automatically be assigned depending on their position on the tower. FUNNELS Funnels take treasure falling in from any direction and send it in the direction of their darkened face.

Funnels showing the direction they will drop the treasure

SWITCHERS Switchers behave like a see-saw. Once they send treasure in the direction of their orange face they switch sides.

A switcher in both states

SPLITTERS Splitters turn one treasure piece into 3 and drop one piece out of each face on their bottom half.

A splitter will multiply treasure and drop in all directions

DEATHTRAPS Deathtraps destroy any treasure that is unlucky enough to fall their way.

A deathtrap destroys any treasure that enters it

Once the tower is built, it’s time for the treasure to tumble! In turn, players must first take the spawner off the tower and set it aside, then remove a chunk of 3 Blinks anywhere from the tower. This chunk must be removed with only one hand, and cannot cause the tower to fall.

Three Blinks and the Spawner are removed from the tower

Then the player must add those 3 Blinks back to the tower one at a time in any configuration as long as the tower doesn’t collapse, with the goal of building a path that directs treasure to their bucket.

All three Blinks are reattached, and the spawner is placed at the highest point

Once the tower is re-assembled, the spawner is added back to the tower. The spawner must be placed at the highest point currently on the tower, so plan your reconstructing appropriately. Once the treasure tumbles down to the bottom and the buckets have collected their treasure, the next player’s turn begins.

Win Conditions

When the bucket is full it will turn purple - the first player to fill their bucket with treasure is the winner!

A bucket fills with treasure, then turns purple

However, if one of the players causes the tower to collapse, they must empty all the treasure from their bucket. Double-click a bucket to empty it. Then the first stage of the game is repeated and the tower is reassembled before treasure is allowed to tumble again.
