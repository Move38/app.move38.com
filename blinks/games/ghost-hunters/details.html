Instructions

Ghost-Hunters You and your friends have decided to go explore the haunted mansion at the end of the street. Armed with nothing but some modified flashlights, can you keep the ghosts at bay? SETUP Blinks will begin in the level select phase, where each Blink will show the level currently selected by the number of faces illuminated. Arrange your blinks in any configuration to be the mansion floor and single click any Blink to toggle through the level options. First-time players should definitely start at level 1.

Single-click to change level from 1-6

Once youâ€™ve selected a level, each player should remove a Blink from the mansion floor and double click it to turn it into a gadget.

A Blink becomes a gadget

To begin play, double click any Blink connected to the mansion floor.

All connected Blinks turn into the dark mansion floor

GAMEPLAY During gameplay, you must blast ghostly apparitions with your gadget and stop them from overtaking the mansion. To zap an apparition, attach your gadget to any exposed side of the mansion - a beam of energy will automatically shoot out in a straight line into the mansion, destroying any spirits in its path.

A gadget shoots a line of light into the mansion.

Your gadget has 3 different functions. To switch between them, single-click your gadget.

Single-click to cycle between the 3 gadget functions

There are four types of apparitions that may appear in the mansion, and each one can only be killed by a specific color gadget functon. GHOSTS Ghosts appear white, and can be destroyed with the white flashlight.

A flashlight destroys a ghost

GHOULS Ghouls appear red, and can be destroyed with the red laser.

A laser destroys a ghoul

GPOLTERGEISTS Poltergeists appear blue, and can be destroyed with the blue GeistLight.

A GeistLight destroys a poltergeist

GOBLINS Goblins appear green, and are only destroyed when they are hit with two different-colored beams at once.

A flashlight and a laser combine to destroy a goblin

Win Conditions

If an apparition fully materializes in the mansion before you zap it, the mansion will become infested with demons and you lose the level.

A mansion overrun with spirits

However, if you are able to ward off spirits for a whole minute then the house will be cleansed and glow with a holy yellow light.

A mansion successfully exorcised!

You may then double-click any Blink to return to level select, and pick a new level to battle the ghosts again.
