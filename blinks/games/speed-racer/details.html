Instructions

SETUP: Separate the Blinks so each piece resets to a loose road, indicated by spinning orange lights. Connect the Blinks together in a single line, then double-click any Blink to create a playable road.

Road setup process

Click any connected road piece to start a car. Single clicks will create standard cars, and multi-clicks (3 or more) will create faster and faster cars. GAMEPLAY: Cars will travel from Blink to Blink, constantly increasing in speed. If your car reaches the end of the road, it will crash in a fiery wreck.

Car traveling and crashing

To keep your car from crashing, extend the road in front of the car using available Blinks. Extend your road by connecting loose roads at the endpoint indicated by a red light. You must connect to the red sideconnecting to any other side will not create a thru road.

There is no finish line in Speed Racer - eventually, every car crashes. The question for you is how far and how fast can you go?
