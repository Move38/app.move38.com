Instructions

FORGET-ME-NOT Forget-Me-Not is a memory game that contains a total of 72 stages. At each stage, your Blinks will display a variety of colors or patterns, before going dark. Keep your wits about you- when the colors reappear, one Blink will have changed. Choose the changed Blink to move onto the next stage. With each stage, the game will become increasingly difficult. Take a deep breath. Recall the past. SETUP Assemble seven Blinks in the shape of a flower blossom; one Blink in the middle, surrounded by 6.

The middle Blink will fade to yellow and sparkle, indicating it is ready to bloom.

Single-click the middle Blink. The flower will bloom.

Single-click the center Blink to begin

The surrounding Blinks (or petals) will display a configuration of colors for a short moment. The colors will then disappear, and reappear. One petal has changed.

The colors will disappear and reappear. Which petal has changed?

Single-click the correct blink, and the flower will turn green. The middle Blink will turn yellow and sparkle, indicating that the flower is ready to bloom once more.

Single-click the Blink that has changed

SCOREBOARD The Blinks will display a scoreboard indicating how many stages you have completed. Click the middle Blink to begin again. The score is displayed by a white pip that will rotate clockwise around the outer faces of the flower, and it will display a count of the completed stages. First, the flower will display red, indicating that it is counting from a range of 1 to 18. If you have completed more than 18 stages, the flower will fade to orange. After the whole flower changes color, the white pip will continue to rotate, now counting from 19 to 36 and so on. There are a total of 4 colors which will display in the below order depending on which range your score falls within: Red is 1-18, Orange is 19-36, Yellow is 37-54, Green is 55-72.
