Instructions

Congrats! You’re responsible for hosting a Blinks Dinner Party. We’ve taken care of the catering-- we just need you to handle the Blinks. Can you keep the extroverts clustered together, and the introverts separate from the group?

PRE-SETUP:

Take a deep breath. Everything is OK.

SETUP:

Group Therapy has no setup - all pieces are always actively playing the game. You simply need to decide when to start participating.

GAMEPLAY:

Each round starts when the Blinks display a shimmery white, purple, and pink pattern - when they do, cluster ‘em together and get ready.

A cluster of Blinks getting ready to begin the game

Then all of a sudden… boom! They’ll reveal their personalities, and you have around 4 seconds to rearrange them. Pink Blinks, the Extroverts, must connect to at least one neighbor

Happy and unhappy Extroverts

Purple Blinks, the Introverts, must have no neighbors

Happy and unhappy Introverts

And that’s how you host a good dinner party! Keep those extroverts clustered together, and introverts separated. At the end of each dinner party, happy Blinks will spiral white, and unhappy Blinks will flash red. Keep those Blinks happy! You’re a party-planning natural.

A completed dinner party with results

Win Conditions

There’s no end game. Play until you feel your worries have melted away.

MULTIPLAYER:

Add more Blinks and play cooperatively with a friend! We HIGHLY recommend playing this game with others!
