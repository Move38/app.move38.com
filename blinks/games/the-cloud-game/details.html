Instructions

SETUP The game will automatically load into its starting state: a clear, blue sky. Begin by clicking any Blink to create a cloud.

GAMEPLAY You have unlimited time and turns to solve a puzzle in The Cloud Game. Click a Blink to switch between two states: Cloud (white) and Sky (blue). Clicking a Sky Blink will change it to a Cloud Blink and vice-versa.

Single-click any blink to shift its state

When clicking a Blink to change its state, all adjacent Blinks will also switch states.

Adjacent blinks will shift states

Win Conditions

The game is won when the sky is full of clouds- when all Blinks on the board are white. Solve the puzzle and silver lining appears, and orange sunspots begin to sparkle through.

Winning display

If you want to reset the puzzle, double click any Blink to revert the entire board to a clear, blue sky.

Double-click any blink to reset the puzzle
